FAQ

Quick answers

Short, plain-language answers to the questions most likely to come up.

Where is my health information stored?

Your information is stored on your device. The app is built so your records stay under your control instead of being kept in a central cloud database.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes. The app is designed for offline use so the important information is still available when signal or Wi-Fi is weak or missing.

Can I set it up for someone else?

Yes. Many people use it for a spouse, child, parent, or another loved one who needs help keeping medical information organized.

Can I get help with accessibility settings?

Yes. If you need help with screen readers, large text, contrast, haptics, or other accessibility features, email support and describe what you need.

How do I contact support?

Email support@myrecordsmyhealth.com. Include your question and, if helpful, a short description of what you were trying to do.

Where can I read the privacy policy?

You can read it here: privacy-policy.html.

Important note

MyRecordsMyHealth helps you organize and present your personal medical information. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace licensed medical care.
