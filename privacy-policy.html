Privacy Policy

MyRecordsMyHealth

MyRecordsMyHealth is designed to help people keep important medical information available when it is needed most. The app is built around local control. MyRecordsMyHealth does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal or medical information with the app operator.

Operator: My Body Is My Health Effective date: April 22, 2026 Support: support@myrecordsmyhealth.com

1. Summary

MyRecordsMyHealth does not collect user data from the app for operator use. Information you choose to enter into the app is intended to stay under your control on your device.

In plain language: we do not collect your personal information, we do not receive your medical records, and we do not build profiles about you.

2. Information collection

MyRecordsMyHealth does not collect personal information, medical information, account information, analytics data, or advertising profiles from you through normal app use.

  • No user health records are transmitted to us through the app.
  • No medical profile data is stored on our servers by the app operator.
  • No sale, rental, or brokered sharing of user information takes place.
  • No third-party advertising trackers are used to profile users inside the app experience.

3. Information you enter into the app

You may choose to enter information such as medications, allergies, diagnoses, physicians, emergency contacts, insurance details, notes, language preferences, or other medical record details.

That information is for your use inside the app and is intended to remain on your device unless you personally decide to export it, show it, copy it, or share it.

4. Storage

MyRecordsMyHealth is intended to use local device or browser storage so that your information remains available to you on the device you use. The app operator does not collect that stored information from you as part of normal app use.

5. Sharing

MyRecordsMyHealth does not silently send your information to us or to outside parties. Information leaves your control only if you choose to share it yourself, such as by displaying it, copying it, emailing it, printing it, or otherwise exporting it using your own device tools.

6. Permissions and device features

If you choose to use app features that rely on device permissions, those permissions are used only so the feature can work on your device.

  • Camera: to capture images or scan relevant information
  • Photo library: to import images you select
  • Device security features: to help protect access on supported devices

Use of these features does not change the policy above: MyRecordsMyHealth does not collect your information for operator storage or profiling.

7. Cookies, analytics, and tracking

We do not use the app to collect analytics or advertising profiles about you. This privacy page is provided as a simple informational page so users, families, and app stores can review the policy in a public location.

8. Your control

Because your information is intended to remain on your device, you stay in control of it. You can update or remove information in the app, and you can delete locally stored app data by removing content from the app or uninstalling it from your device.

9. Children’s privacy

MyRecordsMyHealth is not designed to collect personal information from children through the operator’s systems. If a parent, guardian, or caregiver uses the app to store information for a child or dependent, that information is intended to remain under the user’s local control.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a revised effective date.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact support@myrecordsmyhealth.com.
