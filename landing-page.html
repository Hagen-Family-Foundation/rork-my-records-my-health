Frequently asked

Questions families often ask before they act

Who is this for?

MyRecordsMyHealth is written for seniors, caregivers, people with disabilities or special needs, and anyone managing multiple conditions, physicians, prescriptions, or emergency details.

What makes it different from a generic notes app?

It is organized around medical readiness: medications, allergies, diagnoses, emergency contacts, wallet card value, multilingual communication, and faster understanding in urgent moments.

Why highlight the wallet card and the app together?

Because the combined message is stronger: the app keeps full information available, while the wallet card helps point people to essential details quickly when time is short.

Why mention multilingual communication?

Because language barriers can slow care and increase stress. Making vital information easier to understand across languages is part of what makes the tool distinctive.

What happens after purchase?

Users move into a calm onboarding flow where they add medications, allergies, diagnoses, emergency contacts, physician information, language preferences, and other details that make the record more useful.
