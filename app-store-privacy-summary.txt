MyRecordsMyHealth App Store Privacy Summary
Operator: My Body Is My Health

Based on the current behavior of MyRecordsMyHealth, which is operated by My Body Is My Health:

1. Data collected by the developer
- The app does not collect users’ health record data on developer-operated servers.
- No advertising SDKs are used.
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
- No account creation or login is required.

2. Data linked to the user
- None by the developer, based on the current app behavior.

3. Tracking
- No tracking.

4. Sensitive data handling
- Health and medical information entered by the user is stored locally on the user’s device.
- Document images added by the user are stored locally.
- Optional biometric authentication is used only for local device unlock and is handled by the operating system.
- Exports are user initiated only, such as copying to clipboard or opening the email client.

5. Permissions used
- Camera: photograph medical documents and scan barcodes or QR codes
- Photo library: import document images
- Biometrics: optional local unlock on supported devices

6. Suggested App Privacy position for Apple review
- Data Not Collected, based on current app behavior and no developer-side collection of user health data.

7. Important note
- If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting, customer support chat, or any server-side storage, this summary should be updated before release.
